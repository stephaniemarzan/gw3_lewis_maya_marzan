About the Show

Premiering on September 22, 1994, the American comedy sitcom, Friends, follows a group of single friends through their mid-twenties and early thirties as they try to navigate life in Manhattan, New York together. Throughout the 10 seasons of the show, viewers join the group in special places like their favorite coffee shop, Central Perk, Monica’s apartment, and Joey and Chandler’s apartment across the hall as they experience holidays, career issues, romantic misadventures, family drama, and just friends hanging out.

Friends is held together by six main characters who are all connected to each other through each other. Monica is the scrappy and compulsive younger sister to Ross who tends to be insecure but well-intentioned. Chandler, the sarcastic jokester, was roommates with Ross in college but is now roommates with Joey, the somewhat dim-witted struggling actor. Phoebe, the eccentric musician is part of the group by being a prior roommate of Monica’s before Rachel. Both Monica and Rachel were best friends in high school and Ross always had a secret crush on her. She becomes the sixth member of the group after deciding to leave her impending marriage and kept lifestyle to stay with Monica and make it on her own in the city. This is where the story begins.
